About Us


Kuilsriver Care Village is a nursing home that provides care for elderly individuals. It was established as a non-profit organization in 1976, originally operating as a hospital before transitioning into a nursing home.

The services we offer include personalized care programs tailored to meet the specific needs of the residents, as well as a sick bay for medical attention when required.

The facility employs professional staff members who are responsible for administering medication to elderly patients.

Additionally, we host weekly church services that residents can participate in. However, the nursing home lacks significant online presence to attract new visitors to their online platforms.
