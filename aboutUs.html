About Us


Kuilsriver Care Village, also known as AGS Kuilsriver Tehuis, has been a pillar of compassionate care in our community since 1976. Originally founded as a hospital, we transitioned into a specialized nursing home, driven by the growing need to provide dedicated care for the elderly. As a non-profit organization, our mission is to enhance the quality of life for every resident, offering a safe, supportive environment where they can thrive.

At Kuilsriver Care Village, we provide personalized care programs that are tailored to meet the unique needs of each individual, ensuring that residents receive the attention and support they deserve. Our facility is equipped with a dedicated sick bay for medical care, ensuring prompt attention for any health-related concerns. We pride ourselves on our team of professional and compassionate staff who are skilled in medication administration and elder care, fostering both physical well-being and emotional comfort.

In addition to our core services, we recognize the importance of spiritual nourishment and community engagement. Weekly church services are held on-site, providing residents the opportunity to participate in meaningful spiritual activities, regardless of their physical limitations.

Though our primary focus is on delivering quality care, we are actively working on expanding our online presence to reach more families in need of reliable elderly care services.
